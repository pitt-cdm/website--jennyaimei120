Nassau, Bahamas

We took the trip to the Bahamas a few times. This time, we went after I graduated from high school in 2016. There were many shore excursions, like shopping, snorkeling, and universal park. It took about 3 days to get to the Bahamas. Like any cruise, the staff have lots of activies you can do while on board. And dont forget about the unlimited food!
